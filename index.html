Frequently Asked Questions

What’s included?

All of our websites and web apps will work well and look great on any screen, browser, or device. Websites are built with a content management system so you can create and change your site as needed. We include powerful analytics with everything we build for collecting data & understanding what your users need. Our code is fast, lightweight, and will run flawlessly under pressure.

What about security?

We make sure every piece of data transferred between you and your customers is encrypted with transport layer encryption. In our apps and on our servers we utilize CSRF protections, input sanitization, automatic upgrades, permissions checks, whitelists, blacklists, and other measures to keep your server safe from attackers.

How much does a basic website cost?

A basic, barebones website typically costs between $2,000 - $3,000. We also offer discounts to organizations dedicated to social justice

What makes you different than other agencies or studios?

Most shops seperate designers and developers, and often struggle to bridge the communication gap. We're developers who are also skilled designers, and our work reflects our holistic approach to design and development. The result is websites and apps that are beautiful inside and out. We believe this is the future of web development.
